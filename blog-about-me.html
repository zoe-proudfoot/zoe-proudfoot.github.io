Welcome to My Blog :)

I am going to use this space to introduce myself.

Where I'm from and where I've grown up.

I was born right here in Gisborne, Aotearoa New Zealand in 1999. I have lived here for most of my life, while also spending a few portions of my childhood living in Wairoa and Flaxmere, I felt most at home here on the East Coast.
When I finished highschool, I moved down to Napier for two years to chase my creative passion, but wound up moving home to be with my family when we were going through some hard times.

What have I done, and what brought me here to Dev Academy?

As I mentioned before, I have studied the arts before and have a creative background. In 2018, I took this creativity down to the beautiful Hawke's Bay and studied my level 3 & 4 Arts Certificates and spent majority of the next year doing my level 5 Diploma. I came back home near the end of 2019. I spent majority of 2020 in lockdown with the rest of the world. In about September of that year, I got my first job working in the kiwifruit orchards on the outskirts of Gizzy. I spent about three seasons doing that and loved it for the most part, but felt like I was missing something in my life.
I came across Dev Academy via an email from MSD (WINZ) that caught my attention straight away, I did all the neccersary stuff and now, here I am, telling my life's story almost. I have pretty high hopes for this course, but so far in these first two sprints, I have loved learning about HTML. The CSS is where it's at for my inner designer though. I hope that this will help me to channel my creativity into something positive and give me an awesome career as well!

What are my career goals?

At the end of this course, I am hoping to become either a website developer or a website designer. As much as I would absolutely love to own a business doing this on my own, I would be happy to work for a company while I get better and better at this stuff.
