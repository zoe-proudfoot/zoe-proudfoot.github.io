Leaving Jobs for Greener Pastures

Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on that decision now.

For me, a situaution where I've had to make an ethical decision was when I left my last job. With it being a poor growing season, due to it just being rain almost the whole time. I was also bored after doing the same thing for three seasons straight and that boredom was making me miserable. I left for the sake of my mental health, and I believe ethics were at play here. And to this day, I think it was the best decision I could have made. Plus, it brought me here to Dev Academy, which I definetly have no regrets over!

Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

This isn't really anything I've put much thought into. But I guess I have been brought up to know that family is one of the most important things out there. While there are some not so positive things that I have been trying to shake out of my own mindset for the past few years, the positve stuff will always stick.
